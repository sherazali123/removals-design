What is "Moving Day" Service

MIK is now offering a quick and convenient service we call “Moving Day” where you can rent our movers to transport a few items across town. If you’re selling a couch or a TV and just need to transport it from one end of the city to the other, Moving Day is ideal for you. The same day service is very quick, convenient and affordable. The larger the group for Moving Day, the cheaper the cost.

Conditions

  • Only truck rental service and item delivery provided
  • Only offered the last Sunday of each month
  • Only available around the Seoul, Daejeon, and Busan areas
  • Services are only offered within the same city (ex. Daejeon to Daejeon, not Daejeon to Seoul)
  • Can’t ride with the driver
  • The exact delivery times are not set

Process

Visit our website at http://www.moveinkorea.com and fill out a truck rental service form. (Please make sure that you have requested the last Sunday of the month.)
